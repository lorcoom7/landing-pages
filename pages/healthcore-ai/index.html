Features

Everything your clinical AI team needs

From prototyping with synthetic patients to post-deployment monitoring at scale.

🩺 EHR-native prompt editor
Connect directly to FHIR APIs and test prompts with real de-identified patient data. Multi-modal support for lab values, imaging reports, and clinical notes — all in one workspace.
🧬 Automated clinical evaluations
Run evaluations against curated clinical benchmarks: sensitivity, specificity, AUC across patient subgroups. Detect bias before it reaches a patient.
🚀 Staged deployment with rollback
Ship to 1% → 10% → 100% of clinical users with built-in approval gates. Instant one-click rollback if any metric deviates from threshold.
📡 Live trace monitoring
Full trace visibility for every inference — latency, token costs, hallucination flags, and clinician override rates in real time. HIPAA-compliant audit log for every call.
🤝 Clinician feedback loop
Collect structured feedback from physicians directly inside the workflow. Feed disagreements back into evaluation datasets to drive continuous improvement.
